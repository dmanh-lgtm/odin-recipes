Description

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish consisting of small hand-sized corn or wheat tortillas topped with a filling. They are versatile, easy to make, and perfect for a quick, flavorful meal.

There are many variations, but the most popular version features seasoned ground beef, fresh vegetables, and a squeeze of lime, all tucked into a crispy or soft shell.

Ingredients

  • Protein: Seasoned ground beef or grilled steak.
  • Shells: Hard taco shells or soft corn tortillas.
  • Toppings: Shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and onions.
  • Dairy: Shredded cheddar cheese or crumbled cotija.
  • Garnish: Fresh cilantro, lime wedges, and salsa or sour cream.

Steps

  1. Prepare Filling: Brown the meat in a skillet over medium heat and stir in taco seasoning.
  2. Warm Shells: Heat the tortillas in a pan or crisp the shells in the oven until warm.
  3. Assemble: Place a spoonful of meat in the center of each shell.
  4. Add Toppings: Layer with lettuce, cheese, tomatoes, and your favorite sauces.
  5. Serve: Garnish with fresh lime and enjoy immediately.
